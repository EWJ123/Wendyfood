Pani Puri

Pani Puri, also known as Golgappa. It is an iconic Indian street food experience, a perfect bite-sized package of thrilling contrasts.Crispy, hollow spheres of golden-fried dough are expertly cracked open and filled with a savory stuffing of spiced potatoes and chickpeas. The most significant is the pani—an electrifying and aromatic mint-and-tamarind water, brewed with fresh herbs and a hidden kick of chili.

Served from bustling carts, it is a joyful and interactive ritual. Loved for its complex harmony of crispy and soft, spicy and tangy, cool and fiery. Pani Puri is more than a snack—it is a moment of pure, addictive joy on the vibrant streets of India.
