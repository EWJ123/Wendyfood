Mango Sticky Rice

Mango Sticky Rice is one of Thailand’s most famous street desserts, loved for its sweet, creamy taste and refreshing tropical flavor. It’s often sold by street vendors, especially during mango season.It is made with steamed sticky rice mixed with sweetened coconut milk, served with slices of ripe mango, and often topped with a drizzle of coconut cream and crispy mung beans for texture.

Sticky rice has been a staple in Southeast Asia for a long time. In the late 19th century, it gained popularity when paired with mango, coconut milk, and mung beans for added crunch. This combination became a symbol of Thai cooking, showcasing the sweet and savory flavors that characterize many Thai dishes. Today, Mango Sticky Rice is enjoyed by locals and visitors alike, making it a lasting symbol of Thailand’s street food culture.
